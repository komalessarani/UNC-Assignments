what is a Fairy?

a fairy is a magical creature that resembles a human. Beyond that, every fairy comes in different colors, sizes, and temperaments. Their legend is as old as European civilization itself, no two fairy encounters are quite the same! To learn more about their physical description and characteristics, visit the About page!

intro into Legends

sometimes fairies are described as assuming the guise of an animal. For instance, in Scotland, it was peculiar to the fairy woman to assume the shape of deer while witches became mice, hares, cats, etc. Along with Scotland, Ireland also has its own fair share of fairy legends and lores. To learn more about those legends, visit the Legends page!

intro into Literary Appearances

fairies started appearing in medieval romances as one of the beings that a knight errant- a knight that wanders the land in search of adventures to prove his chivalric virtues - might encounter.
